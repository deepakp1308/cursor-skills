User Composition: Existing Accounts, Not New Customers

Over 90% of both ChatGPT and Claude connections on Mailchimp are existing accounts. Of the connected user base, Claude skews more toward paid plans (63.4% paid) than ChatGPT (52.6% paid) — both far above any other Mailchimp integration channel (Shopify: 10%, Canva: 18%). These are power users adding AI tools to active accounts, not new customers discovering Mailchimp.

Growth Lever: Embedding the Analytics Agent in ChatGPT & Claude Opportunity

Mailchimp's GenAI analytics features — Marketing Analyzer (campaign insights), Segmentation AI (natural-language segment creation), and CJB Smart Draft (automation content) — show high adoption and strong payoff among eligible users. Today, these features are only available inside the Mailchimp app for paid users. Bundling them into the ChatGPT and Claude connector experience would unlock significant incremental value.

The case for embedding: Today, 1,743 ChatGPT users and 1,322 Claude users (~60% and 49% of their bases) are on free plans with no access to Marketing Analyzer, Segmentation AI, or Smart Draft. Their segment-creation rate is 24–31% and email-send rate is 38–54%. If the analytics agent were surfaced through the ChatGPT/Claude connector — even for free users — the data suggests these rates could 2–3× to approach the 67–70% segment creation and 89% email send rates seen among GenAI-eligible users.

Strategic Implication

The AI connectors deliver strong engagement and Automations adoption among existing paid users — a genuine competitive advantage over QBO. But they are failing completely as acquisition channels: zero meaningful paid conversion from new signups, which even the weakest traditional channel achieves at 7–10%.

The analytics agent is the bridge. Embedding Marketing Analyzer and Segmentation AI into the ChatGPT/Claude experience could simultaneously (1) double payoff actions among the 3,065 free-tier connector users, (2) create a natural upgrade trigger by letting free users experience AI-powered segmentation, and (3) push Standard-tier users toward Premium-level engagement (from ~64% to ~90% segment creation). This makes the analytics agent the single highest-leverage investment for turning these channels from engagement-only tools into revenue contributors.

Claude is the stronger channel on every forward-looking metric (velocity, engagement, Automations attachment), while ChatGPT is declining and should be monitored for intervention. The key decision: embed the analytics agent to accelerate payoff and fix the free-to-paid funnel, rather than accepting zero conversion as the status quo.
