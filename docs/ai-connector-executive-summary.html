How the Analytics Agent Accelerates Connector Performance

The ChatGPT and Claude connectors have built a highly engaged user base with strong Automation and SMS adoption. Mailchimp's Analytics Agent — Marketing Analyzer, Segmentation AI, and CJB Smart Draft — currently lives only inside the Mailchimp app. Embedding it in the connector experience would compound the connectors' existing strengths and close their remaining gaps in three critical areas.

1. Closes the Gap on Email Sends & Segment Creation

The connectors excel at engagement and automation adoption. Their one remaining gap is email sends and segment creation — the payoff actions the Analytics Agent is purpose-built to drive. Among connector users, the impact of having agent access is unambiguous:

The agent doesn't replace what the connectors do well — it compounds it by turning already-engaged users into prolific creators of targeted campaigns and audience segments.

2. Compounds the Retention Advantage

Connector users already retain better than the Mailchimp platform average. The Analytics Agent strengthens this by deepening the product usage that keeps users connected.

The Analytics Agent drives exactly the behavior that keeps users retained and connected.

Users who actively engage with analytics-driven segmentation upgrade to higher-tier plans at significantly higher rates, and the agent dramatically improves free-to-paid conversion.

Strategic Implication

The ChatGPT and Claude connectors have built Mailchimp's most engaged user base — leading on WAU, Automations, and SMS adoption. The Analytics Agent is the accelerant that compounds these strengths and closes the remaining gaps.

Embedding the Analytics Agent in the connector experience would: (1) close the email and segment creation gap with a demonstrated 2–3× lift, (2) deepen retention by driving segment-creation behavior that reduces disconnect rates by 40%, and (3) accelerate upgrades — active segmenters upgrade Standard→Premium at 3.7×, and free users with agent access convert to paid at 29% vs 12% without it.

Claude is the stronger channel on every forward-looking metric. Prioritize embedding the Analytics Agent in Claude first — it has the fastest-growing, most-engaged user base and the highest ceiling for incremental impact.
