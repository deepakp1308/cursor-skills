How the Analytics Agent Accelerates Connector Performance

The ChatGPT and Claude connectors have built a highly engaged user base with strong Automation and SMS adoption. Mailchimp's Analytics Agent — the Marketing Analyzer — currently lives only inside the Mailchimp app. Embedding it in the connector experience would compound the connectors' existing strengths and close their remaining gaps in three critical areas.

1. Closes the Gap on Email Sends & Segment Creation

The connectors excel at engagement and automation adoption. Their one remaining gap is email sends and segment creation — the payoff actions the Analytics Agent is purpose-built to drive. Among connector users, the impact of having agent access is unambiguous:

Connector users with the Marketing Analyzer create segments at 2.6× the rate, send emails at 2× the rate, and deliver 3.2× more emails per user. The Marketing Analyzer doesn't replace what the connectors do well — it compounds it by turning already-engaged users into prolific creators of targeted campaigns and audience segments.

2. Compounds the Retention Advantage

Connector users already retain better than the Mailchimp platform average. The Marketing Analyzer strengthens this further — users who engage with its core payoff (segment creation) show measurably lower churn and disconnect rates.

The Marketing Analyzer drives exactly the segment-creation behavior that keeps users retained and connected to the platform.

3. Drives Plan Upgrades & Free-to-Paid Conversion

Users who actively engage with analytics-driven segmentation upgrade to higher-tier plans at significantly higher rates, and the agent dramatically improves free-to-paid conversion.

Strategic Implication

The ChatGPT and Claude connectors have built Mailchimp's most engaged user base — leading on WAU, Automations, and SMS adoption. The Marketing Analyzer is the accelerant that compounds these strengths and closes the remaining gaps.

Embedding the Marketing Analyzer in the connector experience would: (1) close the email and segment creation gap with a demonstrated 2–3× lift, (2) deepen retention by driving segment-creation behavior that reduces disconnect rates by 40%, and (3) accelerate upgrades — active segmenters upgrade Standard→Premium at 3.7×, and free users with Marketing Analyzer access convert to paid at 99% vs 6% without it.

Claude is the stronger channel on every forward-looking metric. Prioritize embedding the Marketing Analyzer in Claude first — it has the fastest-growing, most-engaged user base and the highest ceiling for incremental impact.
